Airshifter Handbook: Community Radio Manual

Categories: FCC, Featured Radio Resources, guide, manual, radio

This Airshifter Handbook is an introductory manual for Radio Free Urbana, a community radio station in Urbana, IL. This manual contains the history of Low Power FM radio, information on programming and a broad overview of operations at the station. It was created by VISTA member Stephen Fonzo serving at the Independent Media Center in Urbana, IL.

PublicMediaCamp

Categories: conference, media, radio, television
When: 
October 17, 2009 - October 18, 2009
Where: 
Washington, DC
Website: 
http://www.npr.org/blogs/inside/2009/08/publicmediacamp_strengthening.html

On the weekend of October 17th at American University’s campus in Washington DC, NPR, PBS and the AU Center for Social Media will co-host a two-day event that we hope will serve as the kickoff for similar community collaboration events around the country. PublicMediaCamp is going to be organized as an unconference - an event without a rigid, top-down programmatic structure, with the sessions organized by the participants themselves.
