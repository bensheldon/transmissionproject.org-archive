Airshifter Handbook: Community Radio Manual

Categories: FCC, Featured Radio Resources, guide, manual, radio

This Airshifter Handbook is an introductory manual for Radio Free Urbana, a community radio station in Urbana, IL. This manual contains the history of Low Power FM radio, information on programming and a broad overview of operations at the station. It was created by VISTA member Stephen Fonzo serving at the Independent Media Center in Urbana, IL.

Non-Profit Guide to Planning and Running Events: Festivals, Concerts, Fundraisers, Art Shows and More

Categories: Events, Featured Events Resources, guide, how to, manual, planning

This comprehensive Event Planning Guide was created by VISTA members Stephen Fonzo and Nicole Pion at the Independent Media Center in Urbana, IL. It includes advice and tips for Setting the Date, Choosing a Venue/Location, Format, Promotion, Budget, Team & Staff, Catering & Entertainment, Networking, and Documentation.

Volunteer Recruitment Staff Training Manual, Grassroots.org

Categories: Featured Volunteer & Staff Training Resources, manual, recruitment, volunteer
Organization: 
Grassroots.org

This staff training manual, created by Laura Benack for Grassroots.org, is designed to orient a new staff member to why volunteers are important to mission, how they are utilized, where are they recruited, and has examples language for posting volunteer advertisements. Grassroots.org volunteers provided $900,000 of in-kind donations in 2009.

Assistive Technology Manual, DISKovery Center

Categories: assistive technology, AT, Featured Media Justice Resources, manual, technology, tools

The Assistive Technology Manual describes not only tools and products to aid people with disabilities, but also has etiquette and advice for respect in communications with a variety of impairments. This manual was developed by Anita Lie at the DISKovery Center in Los Angeles, CA.

Volunteer Manual, Davis Community Network

Categories: Featured Volunteer & Staff Training Resources, intake, manual, policies, volunteer

This volunteer manual is provided to volunteers in order to orient them to the organization, staff, programs, policies and procedures. It also contains information release and emergency contact forms. This document was prepared by VISTA Rian Graves at Davis Community Network in Davis, CA.
